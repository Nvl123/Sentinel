# 🛡️ Sentinel: Personal Growth & Focus Firewall

**Sentinel** is a powerful Android application designed to help users break through cycles of negative habits and maintain a clean streak of personal growth. It acts as a digital "firewall" for your mind, providing immediate, structural interventions when you face intense urges.

---

## 🚀 Key Features

### 💎 The Clean Streak
- **Persistent Tracking**: Your streak timer continues running even when the app is closed.
- **Accurate Statistics**: Clear visualization of your progress in days, hours, and minutes.
- **Relapse Logging**: A thoughtful way to report a broken streak, including a "Double Verification" system to prevent accidental resets.

### 🛡️ The Firewall Protocols (Jurus)
When an urge hits, Sentinel provides a series of structured protocols to interrupt the cycle:
- **Physical Interruption**: High-intensity physical activity (e.g., Push-ups).
- **Grayscale Mode**: Direct link to Android accessibility settings to reduce visual stimulation.
- **Grounding 5-4-3-2-1**: Interactive sensory mapping to bring you back to the present moment.
- **Migration**: Physical movement to a different environment.
- **Cold Reset**: Thermal shock to reset the nervous system.
- **Future Visualization**: Reflecting on long-term goals vs. short-term impulses.
- **Social Boundary**: Reaching out for support.

### 🏠 Home Screen Widget
- **Live Running Timer**: A real-time `Chronometer` widget that shows your streak directly on your home screen.
- **Resizable Layout**: Customizable widget size to fit your aesthetic preference.
- **Instant Access**: Tap the widget to launch the app immediately.

### 🔒 Security & Privacy
- **Secure Password**: Mandatory login to access your logs and settings.
- **Quick Logout**: A dedicated button to secure your session.
- **Isolation Mode**: The Firewall Overlay blocks background interference, ensuring you focus entirely on the recovery protocol.

---

## 🛠️ Tech Stack
- **Languages**: 100% Kotlin
- **UI Framework**: Jetpack Compose
- **Local Database**: Room for activity logs
- **Persistence**: Jetpack DataStore for streak data and session states
- **Architecture**: MVVM (Model-View-ViewModel)

---

## 📦 Getting Started
1. Clone the repository.
2. Open the project in **Android Studio (Ladybug or newer)**.
3. Sync Project with Gradle Files.
4. Run the app on an Android device or emulator (Min SDK 24).
